## LinkedIn Project Description

**Airline Operations Analytics — End-to-End Data Analytics Project**

I built a complete data analytics project simulating a real airline operations review: analyzing 12,000 flights across 6 airlines and 9 U.S. airports to answer the questions leadership actually asks — where are we losing reliability, which routes drive revenue, and does flight delay really hurt customer satisfaction (surprisingly, in this analysis, it barely does)?

The project covers the full analyst workflow: SQL for business querying (joins, CTEs, window functions), Python (Pandas/NumPy) for data cleaning and feature engineering on an intentionally messy raw dataset, Matplotlib/Plotly for exploratory visualization, and a fully specified Power BI executive dashboard.

Key finding: mechanical issues and crew unavailability — not weather — account for half of all cancellations, meaning the highest-ROI operational fix is internal (maintenance/staffing), not external. Full code, SQL scripts, cleaned dataset, and dashboard design are on GitHub.
